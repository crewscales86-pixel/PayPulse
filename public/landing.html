Features
Everything you need to bill clients
From one-click charges to automated webhook billing — PayPulse handles the payments so you focus on delivering results.
📋
Kanban Pipeline
Drag clients between New, Active, At Risk, and Paused. See who has a card on file and who needs follow-up — all at a glance.
⚡
Auto-Billing Webhooks
Connect your GHL webhook. When an appointment is booked or marked Showed, PayPulse charges the client's card automatically. No manual work.
📊
Metrics Dashboard
30-day revenue chart, daily trigger volume, top customers, and failure rates. Interactive charts that show you exactly where your business stands.
🎯
AI Funnel Builder
Generate qualification funnels for any niche. AI writes the questions, collects leads, and integrates with GHL calendar booking.
💳
One-Click Charges
Fire a charge against any client's saved card with one click. No logging into Stripe or Whop. Full charge history on every client.
🔔
Failed Charge Alerts
When a card fails, PayPulse moves the client to At Risk and sends an alert. Optionally forward to GHL for SMS notification to your team.
